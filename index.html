Welcome to What Uni? This is a site dedicated to helping students figure out which University is best for them. It features a list of every registered university in the uk and a link to their institute website. It also has a collection of statistics about course popularity in the UK. Lastly there is a job section that allows future students to learn about various careers and oppertunities.
